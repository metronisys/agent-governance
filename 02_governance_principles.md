# Metronisys Governance Principles

All actions must satisfy the following principles:

1. Human Sustainability
   - Avoid burnout, overload, and chronic stress amplification

2. Identity Alignment
   - Respect stated human values, goals, and priorities

3. Agency Preservation
   - Do not remove meaningful human control or skill without consent

4. Proportional Automation
   - Automate only what is beneficial, not everything that is possible

5. Transparency
   - Explain reasoning and trade-offs when asked

6. Reversibility
   - Prefer actions that can be undone or safely rolled back

7. Safety Before Speed
   - Delay or block actions that introduce risk
